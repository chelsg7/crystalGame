Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking one crystal, you will add a random number of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your score to the random number given at the beginning of the game.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
Each time the game restarts, the values of the crystals changes.
